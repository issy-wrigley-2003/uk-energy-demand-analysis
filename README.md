# uk-energy-demand-analysis

Overview

This project investigates the relationship between UK electricity demand and economic growth over time.

Historically, economic growth has been closely linked to increases in energy consumption. However, improvements in energy efficiency and thhe transition towards a more service-based economy may have weakened the relationship.

Using UK electricity demand and GDP data, this project examines whethe the UK economy has become less dependent on electricty consumption.

Motivation

I am interested in applying quantitative methods to questions in environmental economics, energy policy and sustainability. 

Thir project was developed to gain experience working with real-world economic and energy datasets while investigating an important policy question related to economic growth and enegy transition.
